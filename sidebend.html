• Back

1. From the same position, keeping your feet flat on the ground or on your footrests, place your palms on your knees.

2. Take a moment to come to stillness and take a deep breath.

3. Then take your left arm straight up to the sky.

4. Tilt your upper body to the right and place your right hand on the chair.

5. Open your chest, look up, and feel the gentle stretch on the left side of your torso.

6. Come back to center. Switch sides and repeat the sides, feeling the stretch on the right side of your torso.
