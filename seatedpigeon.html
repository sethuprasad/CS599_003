1. Sit tall with your feet flat on the ground or on your footrests.

2. Bend your right leg and place your right ankle on your left knee.

3. Place your left hand on your right foot and your right hand on your right knee.

4. Take a moment to feel the stretch in your glutes.

5. If you are unable to bring your right foot to meet your knee, then simply try crossing over your left leg to feel to glute stretch.

6. Switch sides and repeat the steps.
