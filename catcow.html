• Back

1. In your wheelchair, gently place your feet flat on the ground or on your footrests.

2. Sit with a tall spine, even if you have to scoot forward a little bit in your seat.

3. Take a moment to come to stillness and take a deep breath.

4. Then round your back and tuck your chin into your chest, stretching your back. Then look up slightly, arch your back, and open your chest.

5. Continue this flow from Cat Pose to Cow Pose a few times to stretch your spine.
